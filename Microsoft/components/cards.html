Fluent Design System

Card

Cards group related information into a contained surface. Fluent 2 cards use a white/surface background, 1px border, 8px radius, and depth-4 shadow for a clean separation from the page background.

Standard, Interactive, Selected
Three states of the base card: at rest, interactive (responds to hover), and selected (blue border signals active selection).
Standard Card
8px radius · depth-4 · 1px border
Use for static content groups: announcements, profile summaries, data panels. Cards separate content from the background without heavy visual weight.
Interactive Card
Hover → depth-8 + border darkens
3
Click or tap this card. Hover to preview the elevation increase. Used for navigable items — file tiles, product cards, settings panels.
Featured Components
Selected Card
2px accent border · checked icon
The selected state communicates the current selection in multi-select grids. The 2px blue border is the Fluent selection indicator.
Horizontal Card
Image or icon on the left with content on the right. Best for list-style layouts where a thumbnail aids recognition.
