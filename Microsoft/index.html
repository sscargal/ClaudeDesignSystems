Materials

Acrylic & Mica

Fluent's translucency system brings depth and context-awareness to surfaces, connecting UI to the physical world.

Acrylic

Light Material

Translucent background using blur(30px) saturate(125%). Used for transient surfaces: menus, flyouts, panels.

Mica

Dark Material

Deeper blur (blur(60px) saturate(150%)) sampled from the desktop wallpaper. Used for persistent surfaces: titlebars, navigation.
