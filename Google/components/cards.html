Elevated Card

Uses --elevation-1 background (5% primary tint) and --shadow-elevation-1. On hover, shadow increases to elevation 2. Best for content that needs to stand above the background.

Interaction States

Cards with role="button" or tabindex get cursor:pointer and state layers. The ::before pseudo-element with background: --color-on-surface handles hover (8%), focus (12%), and press (12%) states.
