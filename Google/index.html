Cards

3 card types — Elevated, Filled, and Outlined. View full page →

G

Elevated Card

Surface tint · Level 1

Elevated cards use a tinted surface background and a subtle drop shadow to create visual depth above the background surface.

M

Filled Card

Surface container highest

Filled cards use the surface-container-highest color for differentiation without shadow, blending into the background more subtly.

T

Outlined Card

Surface · outline border

Outlined cards use a border to separate content from the background, offering a clean look without elevation or fill.
