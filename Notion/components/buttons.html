Buttons

Notion's buttons are deliberately understated. Ghost is the everyday pattern — visible only when needed. Primary (dark) is reserved for high-stakes actions like Share. Blue text handles most inline primary calls-to-action.

Variants
.btn .btn-primary — Dark background, white text. Used for Share, Publish, Confirm.
.btn .btn-secondary — Bordered, surface background. For Cancel, Back, secondary actions.
.btn .btn-ghost — Transparent until hover. The most common Notion button in toolbars and sidebars.
.btn .btn-blue — No background, blue text. For primary actions within content flows.
.btn .btn-danger — Red background + text. Destructive confirmations only.
Sizes
Three sizes: .btn-sm (28px), default (32px), .btn-lg (36px). Font scales accordingly: 13/14/16px.
States
Disabled: opacity 0.4, pointer-events: none. Focus: box-shadow var(--shadow-focus) with blue ring.
With Icons & Icon-only
.btn-icon removes padding and makes the button square. Works with all variants.
Button Group Pattern
Adjacent buttons with modified border radii form a segmented control for view switching.
