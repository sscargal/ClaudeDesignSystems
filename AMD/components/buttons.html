Components

Buttons

AMD buttons are sharp-cornered, bold, uppercase, and energetic. The primary AMD Red communicates urgency and power. All interactive states include red glow on focus.

Primary — AMD Red

Main call-to-action. Fills with AMD Red (#ED1C24), white uppercase text, 44px height. Hover darkens to red-dark with red glow box-shadow.

<button class="btn btn-primary">BUY NOW</button>

Secondary — Outlined Red

Transparent background, 2px red border, red text. Hover fills with AMD Red and switches text to white. Used for secondary CTAs alongside primary.

<button class="btn btn-secondary">COMPARE</button>

Outline — Surface Fill

Surface-2 background, gray border, white text. Hover lifts to surface-3. Used for tertiary actions, comparison selectors.

Ghost — Text Only

Transparent, label-secondary color, no border. Hover shows surface-1 background. Best for low-emphasis actions.

CTA — Hero Call-to-Action

52px height, extra-bold weight, wide letter spacing. Designed for hero sections and product landing CTAs. Lifts on hover with red XL glow.

RYZEN™ 9 9950X

White — Dark Background

Transparent background with white border and white text. Used as a secondary CTA on hero/dark sections alongside the red primary.

Sub-brand Variants

Each product family has a dedicated button color. Used within product family sections and product cards. Each has a matching glow on hover.

Icon Buttons

Square icon-only buttons for toolbar and nav actions. Width = height.

Button Groups

Combine buttons with .btn-group. Supports mixed variants.
